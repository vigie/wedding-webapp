Off The Grid Twilight Campfire

Kick off the wedding revelry with us on Thursday night!

  • 17:00 - 21:00
  • Main Post Lawn, Presidio
  • Entrance free, bring cash for food and drinks

At this classic SF outdoor event in the historic Presidio, food trucks will be serving an array of tasty treats and cocktails with a background of live music.

We’ll have a fire pit reserved… just bring a blanket or chairs and dress in layers.

Getting There

SFO is the closest airport, OAK is the next closest.

If possible avoid landing between 3:30 and 5:30pm to skip the commute traffic into the city

Take a taxi or Uber straight to your hotel (~$50), or take the BART to downtown SF first and then taxi from there.

We recommend downloading both the Flywheel (taxis) and Uber apps to your phone to make getting around a breeze once in the city.

Where to Stay

There are a ton of convenient options for hotels, here are just a few we like that are near the party venue.

  • The Argonaut, a Kimpton hotel (Fisherman’s Wharf)
  • The Tuscan, a Best Western Hotel (Fisherman’s Wharf)
  • Washington Square Inn (North Beach)
  • The Fairmont (Nob Hill)
  • The Westin St. Francis (Union Square)
  • Do not stay at the Parc 55 or Hilton Union Square
