Santa Cruz Beach Day and Camp Out

Spend the day after the party relaxing at the beach and then camp out under the stars.

Please check back often as we continue to update details on this page.

What

Tamara's parents are hosting a day of fun in the sun at their home a short walk from Manresa Uplands beach.

Plan on swimming/surfing, sunbathing and playing games. We'll keep the party going into the night with fire and music.

You are encouraged to bring or borrow a tent to spend the night under the stars in their yard.

When

Saturday, September 17th, 2016
Noon onwards

Where

Santa Cruz is about 75 miles or an hour and a half south of San Francisco by road.

Tamara's parents live just south of the main town in a rural area near La Selva beach
